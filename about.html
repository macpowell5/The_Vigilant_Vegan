About The Vigilant Vegan

The Vigilant Vegan aims to promote the use of Vegan products made out of vegan materials. This allows the preservation and avoidance of usage of animal resources. We assure that no animals were harmed as we make and distribute our products.

History of our store

A set of college classmates was tasked to make a website, they chose the idea of a clothing store that is only producing vegan products. Years later they looked back to their projects and came up with the idea of making the project a reality. They started canvasing for good materials and design, and how to do the marketing. After years of success, they have managed to establish branch as well as partnership with popular clothing distributors.
